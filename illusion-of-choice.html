The Illusion
of Choice

A philosophical experience — inspired by a semester of questions

Over the past months, you have encountered philosophers who questioned everything: what is real, what is just, what we owe each other, and whether any of us truly choose our own lives.

This site will put you in five situations.
You will make a choice in each one.
Afterward, we will ask whether the choice was really yours.

There are no correct answers here.
Only the ones you were always going to give.
